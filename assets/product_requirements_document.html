Project Overview & Context** * **Project Title:** E-commerce Wishlist Feature (V1) * **Version:** 1.0 * **Date:** October 26, 2023 * **Author(s):** [Your Name], Product Manager * **Context Provided Here:** * *Customer feedback analysis consistently shows that users browse products but often don't purchase immediately. Many express a desire to "save items for later" without committing to a purchase or cluttering their cart. Competitive analysis reveals that leading e-commerce platforms offer a "wishlist" or "save for later" functionality, which contributes to higher return visitor engagement and eventual conversion. Our current platform only allows users to add to a shopping cart, which is seen as a commitment, or rely on browser bookmarks, neither of which provides a centralized, persistent way to track desired items. The strategic goal for Q4 is to increase registered user engagement and conversion rate.* --- ### **2. Problem Statement** * **What problem are we trying to solve?** * Users browsing our e-commerce platform lack a convenient, persistent way to save products they are interested in for future consideration (e.g., for gifting, budgeting, or comparing items later), leading to potential loss of user interest and missed conversion opportunities. * **Who experiences this problem?** * Registered users who are in the browsing or consideration phase of their shopping journey, especially those who don't intend to purchase immediately or are comparing multiple items. --- ### **3. Goals & Objectives** * **What does success look like?** * **Overall Strategic Goal:** Increase overall registered user engagement and improve long-term conversion rates on the platform. * **Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound (SMART) Objectives:** * Increase the average number of distinct products saved per active registered user by 20% within 3 months post-launch. * Improve the conversion rate (Wishlist to Purchase) by 15% for users who have added at least one item to their wishlist, within 6 months post-launch. * Increase return visits to the platform by 10% for users who utilize the wishlist feature, within 3 months post-launch. --- ### **4. Target Audience** * **Who are we building this for?** * **Primary:** All registered users of the e-commerce platform. * **Key Segments:** * **Aspirational Shoppers:** Users who browse for higher-value items they plan to purchase in the future. * **Gift Planners:** Users who save gift ideas for friends and family. * **Price-Sensitive Buyers:** Users who save items to track potential price drops or wait for sales. * **Window Shoppers:** Users who enjoy browsing and collecting interesting products without immediate purchase intent. --- ### **5. Proposed Solution / High-Level Requirements** * **What is the proposed solution?** * Introduce a "Wishlist" feature that allows registered users to save products they are interested in, view them on a dedicated page, and easily move them to their shopping cart for purchase. * **Key Functionality (at a high level):** * Users should be able to add/remove products from their wishlist from product detail pages and category listings. * Users should have a dedicated "My Wishlist" page displaying all saved items. * From the wishlist page, users should be able to move an item to their shopping cart. * The wishlist should display current product information (price, availability). --- ### **6. User Stories / Detailed Requirements** * **As a registered user,** I want to be able to add a product to my wishlist from its product page, so I can save it for later. * **As a registered user,** I want to be able to view all items in my wishlist on a dedicated page, so I can easily find them later. * **As a registered user,** I want to be able to remove an item from my wishlist, so I can manage my saved items. * **As a registered user,** I want to be able to move an item from my wishlist directly to my shopping cart, so I can easily purchase it. * **As a registered user,** I want to see the current price and availability status for items in my wishlist, so I am informed before purchasing. * **As a registered user,** I want an indicator on product pages if an item is already in my wishlist, so I don't add duplicates. * **As a guest user,** when I try to add an item to a wishlist, I want to be prompted to log in or register, so I understand the requirement. * **As an administrator,** I want to see aggregate data on the most wishlisted items, so I can gain insights into popular products. --- ### **7. Success Metrics & KPIs** * **How will we measure success against our objectives?** * Number of unique users utilizing the wishlist feature (added >= 1 item). * Average number of items per user's wishlist. * Conversion rate from wishlist view to purchase. * Frequency of users visiting their "My Wishlist" page. * Increase in overall platform average session duration for wishlist users. * Direct feedback (e.g., CSAT scores related to new feature). --- ### **8. Scope (In & Out)** * **In Scope (What WILL be delivered in this phase/version):** * Ability for registered users to add/remove items to/from a single wishlist. * Dedicated "My Wishlist" page for registered users. * Functionality to move items from wishlist to cart. * Display of current price and stock status on the wishlist page. * Login/registration prompt for guest users attempting to use the feature. * Basic analytics tracking for wishlist activity. * **Out of Scope (What will NOT be delivered in this phase/version):** * Sharing wishlists with other users. * Multiple wishlists per user (e.g., "Holiday Gift List," "My Dream Products"). * Automated price drop notifications for wishlisted items. * Guest user wishlists (requires login). * Social media integration for wishlists. * "Notify me when in stock" functionality for wishlisted items. --- ### **9. Assumptions & Constraints** * **Assumptions:** * Our existing product catalog API is robust and scalable enough to support additional requests for wishlist item details. * Users understand the concept of a "wishlist" from common e-commerce patterns. * The UI/UX design can be implemented without significant performance degradation. * Existing user authentication system is stable and can be integrated. * **Constraints:** * **Timeline:** Target launch date is end of Q4 2023 to capitalize on holiday shopping trends. * **Resources:** Limited to current engineering and design team capacity. * **Technical Debt:** Prioritize minimal impact on existing core e-commerce functionalities. --- ### **10. Open Questions & Dependencies** * **Open Questions:** * Final iconography for "Add to Wishlist" button/action. * Exact placement of the wishlist icon/link in the global navigation. * Behavior/messaging if a wishlisted item becomes unavailable/discontinued. * A/B testing strategy for button placement or UI elements. * **Dependencies:** * **Design Team:** UI/UX mockups, high-fidelity prototypes, interaction flows. * **Engineering Team (Backend):** New database schema for wishlists, API endpoints for CRUD operations. * **Engineering Team (Frontend):** Integration of wishlist functionality into product pages, development of "My Wishlist" page. * **QA Team:** Test plan development and execution. * **Marketing Team:** Communications plan for launch. --- ### **11. Go-to-Market Strategy (High-Level)** * **How will we launch and promote this?** * In-app tooltip/banner upon first login post-launch for registered users. * Email announcement campaign to registered user base. * Update FAQ and help center articles. * Internal communication to customer support team for training. * Small social media announcement. --- ### **12. Future Considerations** * **What might come in future iterations?** * Ability to create multiple, custom-named wishlists. * Sharing wishlists via unique links or social media. * Price drop alerts and "back in stock" notifications for wishlisted items. * Personalized recommendations based on wishlist content. * Integration with gifting services. ---
